# Research Archive

> Stale or low-relevance research items, kept for permanent dedup and audit.

The librarian moves items here per `config.yml.auto_archive` settings:
- `low_relevance_days` (default 30): items with `relevance: low` untouched for N days
- `any_relevance_days` (default 90): any item untouched for N days
- `actioned_days` (default 180): items marked `status: actioned` for N days

Archived files keep their frontmatter and content. They no longer surface in `_meta/` views but are still searchable and contribute to `seen_urls` so we don't re-discover them.

Organized by `<year>/<original-topic>/<original-type>/<file>`.
